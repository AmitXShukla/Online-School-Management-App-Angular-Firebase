Help: Security Rules

ElishOne ERP Data implements very strict security data policy.
At any give point of time, ElishOne ERP Data owner is alway the person who orginally signed up for Pro key.

Whenever, Pro Account owner changes the key, all other user access will immediatly be removed.

At any given time, Pro Account Owner can ask us to delete his organization data by simple emailing us at info@elishconsulting.com

* share your < orgname and Secret key > with any person, and person will have access to all your data.

** Only you can change your secret key (simple browse to settings page and write same orgname with a new key).

*** Changing secret key will remove access to all your existing users who are using your orgname and secret key.
